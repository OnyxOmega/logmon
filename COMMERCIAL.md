# Commercial Licensing

logmon is released under the
[PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0](LICENSE.md) license. That license permits a broad
range of **noncommercial** use — personal, educational, charitable, research,
public-safety, and government use — at no cost.

## When you need a commercial license

You need a separate commercial license if you use logmon for a commercial
purpose, including (non-exhaustively):

- Use by a for-profit company in the course of its business.
- Bundling or distributing logmon as part of a commercial product or service.
- Providing paid services (managed services, consulting deliverables, MSP/MSSP
  offerings) built on or incorporating logmon.

## How to obtain one

Contact the maintainer (YASDC / Kevin Perryman) via
<https://github.com/OnyxOmega/logmon> to discuss commercial licensing terms.

Government institutions should note that governmental use is already a permitted
noncommercial purpose under PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0.
